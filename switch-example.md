### Switch example
```php
$carBrand = "Toyota";

switch ($carBrand) {
    case "Toyota":
        echo "Toyota is a popular car brand known for its reliability and fuel efficiency.";
        break;
    case "Ford":
        echo "Ford is a well-established car brand with a wide range of models and a strong presence in the market.";
        break;
    case "BMW":
        echo "BMW is a luxury car brand renowned for its performance, craftsmanship, and innovative features.";
        break;
    default:
        echo "Car brand not recognized. Please provide a valid car brand.";
        break;
}
```
